Annapurna Administration mobile application | Python[kivy], MySQL, Android

Implemented an android application to manage meal orders of college student who require free food and manage multiple canteens. This app makes it simple to order, track, manage food supplies while also managing billing effectively.

Students Management Data Module

This module is designed to manage and maintain student data for those who require free food in a college or educational institution. The module has three primary functions.

  • uploading data from an Excel file.
  • updating or editing student data by searching for their roll number.
  • viewing and deleting student data from the database.

Canteen Management Data Module

The "Manage Canteen Data" module is designed to manage and maintain data related to the canteen and its users in a college or educational institution. The module has several primary functions.

  • including adding canteen user sign-up data.
  • updating the sign-up data with app rights.
  • viewing live data of food orders by students.
  • viewing and deleting canteen user data.

Canteen Data Reporting

The "Canteen Data Reporting" module is designed to provide efficient and organized reporting functions for canteen administrators in a college or educational institution. This module has three primary functions.

  • including a brief report.
  • a detailed report.
  • an attendance report.
