Implemented Windows software to allocate a seat for students for exams in the exam hall without placing one and another with same exam together in excel format. This software will execute this process with the help of user input of no of seating in a hall and list of students and subjects in excel to the software.

EXAM SEATING SOFTWARE

The Exam Seating Management System (ESMS) is a comprehensive desktop application developed using Python and Tkinter, tailored to optimize the intricate process of student seating during examinations. By intelligently processing student and hall details from Excel files, ESMS generates three distinct reports—Notice Board Report, Question Paper Report, and Seating Report—streamlining the administrative workflow and enhancing the overall efficiency of examination management.

NO OF QUESTION PAPER REPORT

  • Provides staff members with the necessary information to determine the required number of question papers for each room.
  • Ensures efficient distribution of question papers based on the seating arrangement.

NOTICE BORD REPORT

  • A concise summary of seating information for students, including room number and seat allocation.
  • Tailored for easy display on notice boards, facilitating swift communication of seating arrangements to students.

SEATING REPORT

  • Detailed report for staff members containing comprehensive seating information.
  • Includes student name, registration number, room number, subject code, and seat number.
  • Facilitates easy monitoring and management of the examination seating arrangement.
